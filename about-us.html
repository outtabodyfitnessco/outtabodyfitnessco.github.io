About Us

What we have done

At Outta Body Fitness, we've transformed countless lives through our comprehensive fitness programs and personalized training approach. Founded with a passion for helping individuals achieve their health and wellness goals, we've built a community of fitness enthusiasts who have experienced remarkable transformations in both body and mind.

Our certified trainers, led by experienced fitness professional Mike B., combine cutting-edge fitness science with proven training methodologies to deliver results that last. From our state-of-the-art facility in Englewood, Ohio, we've helped clients build strength, improve cardiovascular health, and develop sustainable healthy habits that extend far beyond their workout sessions.
